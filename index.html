Technology & Equipment

State-of-the-Art Treatment Equipment

Our fully-equipped 4-floor clinic features the latest physiotherapy machines for effective, evidence-based treatment.

IFT

Interferential Therapy

Deep tissue pain relief using medium-frequency electrical currents to reduce inflammation, muscle spasms, and chronic pain.

TENS

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation

Non-invasive pain management using low-voltage electrical currents to block pain signals and stimulate endorphin production.

US

Ultrasound Therapy

Deep heating treatment using sound waves to accelerate tissue healing, reduce swelling, and relieve pain in soft tissues.

LLLT

Laser Therapy

Low-Level Laser Therapy for accelerated wound healing, reduced inflammation, and pain relief at the cellular level.

PWT

Paraffin Wax Therapy

Thermotherapy for hand and wrist conditions — reduces stiffness, improves circulation, and relieves joint pain in arthritis.

HT

Heat Therapy

Superficial and deep heating modalities to relax muscles, increase blood flow, and prepare tissues for exercise therapy.

MT

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilization, soft tissue massage, myofascial release, and trigger point therapy.

EX

Exercise Therapy

Therapeutic exercise programs with strengthening, stretching, balance training, and functional movement retraining.

A Decade of Healing in Rajkot

Sunshine Physiotherapy Clinic was established by Dr. Disha Rupareliya Bhate in 2016. Dr. Disha holds a Ph.D. in Physiotherapy and is a Gold Medalist in Masters of Physiotherapy (MPT) specializing in Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy from Gujarat University.

She is also a researcher and author of international publications, bringing the latest evidence-based practices to every patient interaction. The clinic is supported by Dr. Falak Tripathi (BPTh/BPT, 12 years experience) who provides on-call consultations.

Spread across 4 fully air-conditioned floors with state-of-the-art equipment, we treat patients of all ages — from pediatric to geriatric — covering orthopedic, neurological, cardio-respiratory, and sports conditions.
