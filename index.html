About the Clinic

A Rajkot physiotherapy centre built around evidence-based recovery and hands-on care.

Sunshine Physiotherapy Clinic is publicly described on Practo as a private physiotherapy centre established by Dr. Disha Rupareliya Bhate in Rajkot. The clinic listing says the centre spans four floors, is fully air-conditioned, and offers orthopedic, neurology, cardio, maternity, and pain-relief physiotherapy for patients across age groups.

The strongest qualification trail found online identifies Dr. Disha Rupareliya Bhate as MPT (Musculoskeletal), PhD, a Gold Medalist in MPT from Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad, and a GPC 1523 registrant with the Gujarat State Council for Physiotherapy.
